The prior evidence set does not assign numerical confidence scores. This document preserves confidence without inventing a score: a route-specific historical dealer capture is confidence for the exact visible state only; a historical inventory record is lower-confidence route/state evidence only; and an admin-session probe has no confidence for dealer parity. No historical confidence extends to a fresh current-source claim.

1. A future `brp-dev1` pass must use a dealer-authenticated session, save a desktop 1440px and mobile 390px capture (or a dated DOM record) for every route below, and record the resulting date and confidence without overwriting the historical date.

3. A clone control is either enabled with an observable deterministic local effect, or locked with a persistent explanation that works by touch, mouse, and keyboard focus. A title tooltip alone is insufficient.
4. Local order creation, notes, chat, customer/equipment, cart, and a source-confirmed local workshop workflow may change in-memory/browser-local state. They must not represent a remote action as complete.
5. 1C, synchronization, real transfer of files, export/download, approval, shipment, permissions, and operational request submission remain locked pending an approved backend contract.
