# Dealer Secondary Pages Specification

Use one shared FeaturePage grammar: 24px heading, 15px helper, optional KPI row, 36px toolbar, white bordered content card and source-specific empty/populated state.

## Meaningful variants

- Accessories: three families, product grid, filters and product detail dialog.
- Units: four tabs and expandable container HAMU4124410.
- Schedule: 23-slot timeline and detail dialog.
- BossWeb: 9779150 query with BossWeb 0 available / 12 backorder and local catalog 240.
- Workshop: status board and local work-order dialog.
- Consignment: stock/network/request tabs and request preview.
- Settlements: 30/60/90/180/360 period buttons.
- Documents, Drafts, Inventory and Network: observed empty states.

All controls are usable where they affect clone-local filtering/display; controls implying external sync/import are visual only.
